What I like about SASS

  • 2. Improved organization: Sass supports modular code organization through partials and importing, making it easier to manage and reuse stylesheets across different parts of a project.
  • 3. Mathematical calculations: Sass allows for mathematical operations within stylesheets, facilitating responsive design and complex layout calculations.
